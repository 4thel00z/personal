A CMS is great when you need complex editorial workflows. But for most product blogs, a folder of `.mdx` files gets you 90% of the value with 10% of the complexity.

## The simple model

- One file per post: `website/app/blog/posts/my-post.mdx`
- A small `meta` export for title, description, date, and tags
- A loader that discovers posts and renders them

## Benefits

- Everything is reviewed like code.
- Writing works offline.
- Refactors are trivial (rename a tag, fix a typo, update links).
